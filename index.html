About Us

Education Is The Foundation

Get your child learning and thriving at a young age! At Ivy Montessori Academy, we provide quality education and care for your infants, toddlers, or young child. We have been providing Montessori education for more than 15 years.Through our passion for Montessori education and our dedication to complete preparedness, our goal is to help every child become academically, socially, and emotionally ready for elementary school and the rest of their lives.

What We Provide

  • We accept infants from 6 weeks and above and provide the needed care through our experienced Infant Care teachers.
  • We help our children through the process of Potty training.
  • We take minimal holidays for the convenience of our working parents and have a fun and educational summer program for all ages.
  • We have excellent outdoor facilities for our children to play in, and if the weather doesn’t permit, we have great indoor facilities as well.
  • We provide nutritious and fresh food in the morning, at lunch, and evening at no additional cost!
  • We prepare your children for academic success now and later.
  • Health and safety is our top priority.
